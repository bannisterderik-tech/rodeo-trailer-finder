Privacy Policy

Effective July 2026
Template notice. Haulin' Hooves is an early demo prototype. This policy is a good-faith template provided for the beta test and is not legal advice. Have qualified counsel review it before you rely on it in production, and replace the bracketed contact details with your own.

This Privacy Policy explains how Haulin' Hooves ("we," "us," "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our demo prototype and related web pages (the "Service") and when you sign up to test the beta.

1. Information We Collect

Information you give us

  • Beta signup details: your name, email address, and (optionally) phone number when you request access to the demo.
  • Feedback: ratings, comments, and any contact details you choose to include when you submit feedback.
  • Rig & trip preferences you enter (trailer dimensions, saved venues, starting location, and similar). In this demo these are stored in your own browser and are used to power the prototype.

Information collected automatically

  • Basic technical data such as device/browser type and general usage of the demo, to the extent your browser or our hosting provider records it (e.g., server logs). We do not use third-party advertising trackers in this prototype.
  • Local storage on your device is used to remember your demo inputs and whether you have completed the signup. You can clear this at any time from your browser settings.

2. How We Use Information

  • To give you access to the demo and communicate with you about the beta.
  • To respond to your feedback and improve the product.
  • To maintain security, prevent abuse, and comply with legal obligations.

We rely on your consent (which you give at signup) and our legitimate interest in operating and improving a demo product as the bases for these uses.

3. How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information, and we do not share it for cross-context behavioral advertising. We may share information with:

  • Service providers (for example, form/email or hosting providers) that process data on our behalf under appropriate confidentiality terms.
  • Legal & safety recipients where required by law or to protect rights, safety, and property.
  • Business transfers in connection with a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, subject to this policy.

4. Data Retention

We keep beta signup and feedback data for as long as needed to run the beta and then delete or de-identify it. You may ask us to delete your information at any time (see "Your Rights").

5. Security

We use reasonable administrative and technical measures to protect information. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or port your personal information, and to withdraw consent. To exercise any right, email us at hello@haulinhooves.com. We will respond as required by applicable law and will not discriminate against you for exercising your rights.

California residents have additional rights described in our California Privacy Notice & Do Not Sell or Share page.

7. Children

The Service is not directed to children under 16, and we do not knowingly collect their personal information. If you believe a child provided us information, contact us and we will delete it.

8. International Users

We operate the Service from the United States. If you access it from elsewhere, you understand your information may be processed in the U.S.

9. Changes

We may update this policy as the product evolves. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Effective" date above.
